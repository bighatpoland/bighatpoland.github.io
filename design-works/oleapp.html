GET YOUR HEAD AROUND

Crafted by the innovative minds at Big Hat Poland, our tourist app is your ultimate travel companion. This intuitive and beautifully designed application opens the door to exploring destinations with confidence and ease. Whether you're wandering through bustling city streets or serene landscapes, our app ensures you have all the essential information at your fingertips. Features include real-time navigation, curated lists of must-visit sites, local dining recommendations, and interactive itineraries tailored to your interests. Seamlessly plan your trip, from transportation options to booking accommodations, all integrated within our user-friendly platform. Experience the world like never before with our tourist app – your adventure starts here!

The scope of work was:

  • Initial concept and design approval.
  • Beta testing phase with select users.
  • Launch of the app on both iOS and Android platforms.
  • Post-launch support and updates for six months.
