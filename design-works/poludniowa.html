poludniowa fala / southern wave

The project aims to position Poludniowa Fala as a leading choice for vacation rentals in Debki, enhancing its visibility in the competitive market. By focusing on a strategy that merges local charm with modern amenities, the campaign will attract a diverse range of Polish tourists seeking a memorable seaside experience.

The integration of sustainability and personalized services will set Poludniowa Fala apart, ensuring a strong, loyal customer base and positive brand perception.

The scope of work was: graphics and visual aids, social media platform strategy, content creation, interactive engagement. Digital marketing, marketing materials.
